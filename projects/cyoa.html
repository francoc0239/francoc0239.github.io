CYOA Project

Context

After learing how to code more advanced HTML, CSS, and learning how to use github and its terminal. Our SEP teacher, Mr.Mueller told us to make a CYOA project with two one or two other people that picked the same subject as us. CYOA stands for "choose your own adventure" meaning that you can do anything you want and the story can be about anything you want too. This project was hard but it also allowed us to do anything we want with our project. The project also allowed us to use what we learned throughtout the year in one project and to teach us how to work with others.

Content

Our CYOA project is about fightng our teacher Mr.Mueller. People have the choice to run away or to fight him. No matter what decision you pick, you could lose or you could win. You have to choose wisely because Mr.Mueller is very powerful and you can get killed very fast. It was very fun to make but while making the project I had some challenges that me and my team went throught.

Reflection

Challenges

One challenge we had while coding the project is when we had merging erroes meaning when we pushed and pulled to each other, we got errors because we changed the same line of code or the one next it. In order to fix the error we had to pick what we wanted it to say and push again. we were confused on what to do because it was our first time actually having a merging error. What we did to fix it is to just delete the part that was with the error and added it back but without allthe error messages and the choices. Next we pushed it to the other teammates then they pulled. When we did this, it worked which allowed us to contuine with our project. We learned how to do this all thanks to our teacher teaching us this before we started. It was a challenge because it was our first time actully having a error in our project so we were just scared on what to do.

Another challenge we had while coding is when me and my teammates were confused about why one of us were behind in the project and why one of us was fine. What happened is that my teammate told me that they pushed the work that they did so I had to pull to get the work they did. But when I pulled, nothing changed because I didnt need to. I was confused because they said they pushed but I cant see it. So in order to fix this, I asked my teacher mr. mueller in order to help us. He told us to check our status and when he did, the person that said that they push didn't push. They forgot to commit the push. So because they didn't commit, they couldnt push their work which is why I couldn't see. When they realized that they didn't push, they commited and then pushed. Then I pulled and now I could see the work which allowed us to contuine working on the project without any problems.

Takeaways

One takeaway I can take from challange two is to always ask for help when you really need it. This is because if I never asked for help, we would have been in more trouble if we saved that problem for later, making the project much harder to do. Before I started this project, i would never ask for help because I always felt like I could do it alone and that I had to do it alone in order to actually learn. But getting the courage to ask Mr.Mueller for help and it actually working has helped me realize that there are other ways to learn and they could involve multiple people.

One takeaway I can take from challange one is to always use wewhat you learned into the real world. This is because if Mr.Mueller didnt teach us about merge conflicts, we could still be stuck on them. Mr.Mueller teaching us merge conflicts has helped us. We didn't expect that we would actually have a merge conflict. At first it was a bit confusing, but after using what Mr.Mueller taught us, it was really easy to fix it. It just took a while to took what we learn and use it to fix the problem that we had. This is why I will now use what I learn and not take it for granted. I will also learn how to work faster when faced with a problem.
